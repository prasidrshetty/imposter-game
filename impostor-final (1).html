HOW TO PLAY

Master the art of deception 🕵️

🎯 THE GOAL

Everyone gets a secret word on their own phone.
Most players are Citizens — they all get the same word.
One (or more) are Impostors — they get a similar but different word.

Citizens must find the Impostor. The Impostor must blend in! 🎭

1
Everyone Joins
Host creates a room and shares the 6-letter code. All players join on their own phone using that code.
2
See Your Secret Word
Each phone privately shows your role. Citizens see the word. Impostors see ??? — you don't know the word!
3
Give Clues Out Loud
Everyone takes turns giving one clue about their word. Don't say the exact word. Impostor must fake it!
4
Discuss & Debate
Talk it out! Who sounded suspicious? When ready, host taps Stop → Vote.
5
Vote on Your Phone
Everyone votes privately. Most votes = eliminated. Results reveal who won! 🎉
👥
CITIZENS WIN
Vote out the Impostor correctly
🕵️
IMPOSTOR WINS
Avoid being voted out & survive
💡 PRO TIPS
🟣Impostors — listen carefully to others' clues before giving yours. Mirror their style!
🟢Citizens — give clues specific enough to prove you know the word, but vague enough to not hand it to the Impostor.
🔴Watch for hesitation! A long pause before giving a clue is very suspicious. 👀
🟡Best with 5–8 players for maximum chaos and fun!
📝 EXAMPLE ROUND
Word pair: Car (citizens) vs Bike (impostor)
Prasid: "It has four wheels" ✅
Rahul: "You need a licence for it" ✅
Sneha (impostor): "People use it for daily travel" 🤔
Arjun: "It can go very fast on the highway" ✅

Everyone suspects Sneha — her clue works for both! Vote time... 🗳️
